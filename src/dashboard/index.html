Privacy & Data

Jiminy is designed to be local-first. Your Context Folder is the source of truth. In Cloud mode, generating results from recordings sends still images directly from your Mac to the provider you configure. In Local mode, still processing happens on-device and nothing leaves your machine.

  • Everything is saved under <Context Folder>/jiminy/.
  • Recorded still images: jiminy/stills/.
  • Extracted stills markdown: jiminy/stills-markdown/.
  • Clipboard text (while recording): jiminy/stills-markdown/session-*/<timestamp>.clipboard.txt.
  • Settings file: ~/.jiminy/settings.json.
  • In Cloud mode, Jiminy sends capture content directly to the AI provider you configure (for extraction).
  • Requests go directly from your Mac to that provider (not through a Jiminy server).
  • In Local mode, capture content stays on-device.
  • When enabled, Jiminy captures a still every few seconds while you are active.
  • It stops when your Mac is idle.
  • You can pause for 10 minutes from the tray menu or the Toggle Recording hotkey.
